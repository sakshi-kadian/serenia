### Long Version (Full Description)
Serenia is a comprehensive AI-powered mental wellness platform designed to provide accessible, intelligent therapeutic support through natural conversations. The application employs a sophisticated hybrid AI architecture that combines the clinical precision of specialized BERT-based NLP models with the conversational fluency of Google Gemini AI.

**Technical Architecture:**
The system processes every user message through four specialized NLP modules: (1) Emotion Detection using BERT fine-tuned on GoEmotions dataset identifying 28 distinct emotions with 85% accuracy, (2) Anxiety Classification providing severity assessment across 4 levels with personalized recommendations, (3) Crisis Detection implementing multi-layered keyword analysis and context evaluation for self-harm and suicide ideation, and (4) Context Management tracking emotional trajectories and conversation history. These analyses inform Google Gemini AI's response generation, creating empathetic, context-aware replies that adapt to the user's emotional state.

**Core Features:**
- **Intelligent Chat:** Real-time therapeutic conversations with emotion-aware AI responses
- **Crisis Intervention:** Automated detection and immediate resource provision for users in distress
- **Automated Journaling:** AI-generated reflections analyzing conversation patterns and emotional insights
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Comprehensive mood tracking, anxiety trends, and personalized recommendations
- **Secure Infrastructure:** Clerk authentication, encrypted database, and HIPAA-compliant data handling

**Technology Stack:**
Frontend built with Next.js 14, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS; Backend powered by FastAPI with SQLAlchemy ORM; Database using PostgreSQL via Supabase; AI/ML leveraging Transformers, BERT models, and Google Gemini API; Authentication through Clerk with JWT tokens.

**Impact:**
The platform provides clinical-grade mental health support accessible 24/7, with safety features that can identify crisis situations and provide immediate resources. The automated journaling and analytics features enable users to track their emotional journey over time, while the conversational AI creates a therapeutic experience that feels natural and supportive.

### "Describe a technical decision you made"
"I chose a hybrid AI architecture for Serenia instead of relying solely on Gemini AI. While Gemini excels at natural conversation, it's not specialized for mental health. I integrated BERT models fine-tuned on emotion datasets to provide clinical-grade analysis, then used that data to inform Gemini's responses. This gave us both precision (BERT) and natural language (Gemini), resulting in a system that's both accurate and therapeutic."
